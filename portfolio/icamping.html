Camping is Easy

Icamping makes it easy and delightful for newbie campers to fully embrace the nature with least required preparation, as well as learn about camping tips from a camper community.

We worked closely with the project leads to design their new brand and their service experiences. We managed to create the logo and visual package in a friendly, welcoming, and organic tone. Meanwhile, we accentuated the convenience of service by creating simple user steps, and providing proper suggestion for camp kit options.

Our Goal

Our target was to design simple user flows, and help users order with confidence by giving proper guidance on camp ground, camp kits, and general knowledge.

One-stop Shop

Icamping offers a range of camping equipments. We decreased inputs and steps for the minimal needed information, and provide customers with the best options of camp kit packages. This effectively alleviates users' burden of choosing different kits.

Newbie Guidance

We aimed at helping newbie campers understand basic knowledge and tips, so that they can choose their camp ground and destination with adequate information. We fostered the community by allowing commenting and rating, as well as articles of tips.
